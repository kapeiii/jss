Academic Honors

President’s Lister

  • GWA: 1.0000 – 1.4500
  • No grade 2.50 or higher
  • Complete academic load

Dean’s Lister

  • GWA: 1.4501 – 1.7500
  • No grade 2.50 or higher
  • Complete academic load

Note: Final qualification is subject to official evaluation by the Registrar and College policies.

Latin Honors

Summa Cum Laude

  • GWA of 1.0000 - 1.2500
  • No failing or conditional grades

Magna Cum Laude

  • GWA of 1.2501 – 1.4500
  • No failing or conditional grades

Cum Laude

  • GWA of 1.4501 – 1.7500
  • No failing or conditional grades

Note: Final qualification is subject to official evaluation by the Registrar and College policies.
