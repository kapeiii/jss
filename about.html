Why this app exists and who it’s built for

About This App

This website is not affiliated with or officially part of Bicol University or BU-ICTO. It is a personal project created by a BU-Polangui BS Computer Engineering student to help students compute their General Weighted Average (GWA) accurately and efficiently.

Mission

The mission of this project is to provide a simple, accurate, and user-friendly GWA calculator tailored specifically for Bicol University grading standards—helping students track their academic performance with clarity and confidence.

About Bicol University

Bicol University is the premier state university in the Bicol Region, established on June 21, 1969 through Republic Act 5521. It upholds the core values of Scholarship, Leadership, Character, and Service.
